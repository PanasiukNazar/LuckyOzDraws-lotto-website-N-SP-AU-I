AU Lotto Cookies Policy

Learn how we use cookies to improve your experience on AU Lotto

Last Updated: March 20, 2025

Introduction

AU Lotto ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on this website. This Cookies Policy explains how we use cookies, what types of cookies we use, and how you can control them. Please read this policy alongside our Privacy Policy to understand how we handle your personal data.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files saved on your device when you visit websites. They help AU Lotto provide a smoother, more personalized experience by remembering your preferences and enabling core website functions.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies on AU Lotto for purposes such as:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to work properly, including security and account access features. These cannot be disabled.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences to enhance your AU Lotto experience. These may be set by us or trusted third parties.
  • Performance Cookies: Gather anonymous data on website usage to help us improve AU Lotto’s performance.
  • Marketing Cookies: Help deliver ads relevant to your interests and limit ad frequency.

Types of Cookies We Use

On AU Lotto, we utilize these cookie types:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser, used to maintain session information. Session
Persistent Cookies Stay on your device for a set period or until deleted, helping recognize you on return visits. Up to 2 years
Authentication Cookies Identify logged-in users to secure accounts and personalize features. Session
Analytics Cookies Collect anonymous data on how visitors use AU Lotto to enhance site functionality. Up to 2 years
Marketing Cookies Track online activity to deliver relevant advertising and manage ad frequency. Up to 2 years

Third-Party Cookies

AU Lotto’s website includes cookies set by trusted third parties such as analytics and advertising providers. These third parties collect information to deliver tailored ads and measure their effectiveness.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage cookies through your browser settings by:

  • Deleting cookies from your device
  • Blocking cookies through browser settings
  • Receiving notifications when cookies are set

Please note that blocking or deleting cookies may affect your ability to use certain AU Lotto features.

For more information about cookies and how to manage them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

Updates to This Cookies Policy

AU Lotto may update this Cookies Policy as technology and regulations change. Changes will be posted here with the revised date. Please review periodically to stay informed.
