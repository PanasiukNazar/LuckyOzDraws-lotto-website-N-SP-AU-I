Interpretation and Definitions

Interpretation

Words with initial capital letters carry specific meanings defined here. These definitions apply equally in singular or plural form.

For this Privacy Policy:

  • Account refers to your unique profile created to access AU Lotto services.
  • Affiliate means an entity that controls or is controlled by the Company through ownership or management.
  • Company (also "We", "Us", or "Our") refers to luckyrollbets Australian Lottery, luckyrollbets.com.

  • Device means any equipment used to access the Service, such as a computer, smartphone, or tablet.
  • Personal Data includes any information identifying you personally.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

When participating in AU Lotto, we may request certain personal information to identify and contact you, including but not limited to:

  • Email address
  • First and last name
  • Phone number
  • Address details such as city, state, and postal code
  • Usage Data related to your interaction with the AU Lotto service

Usage Data

We automatically collect Usage Data when you interact with AU Lotto.

This data may include your device’s IP address, browser type and version, pages visited, visit timestamps, time spent on pages, unique device identifiers, and diagnostic information.

When accessing AU Lotto via a mobile device, additional data such as your mobile device type, unique device ID, operating system, and browser type may be collected automatically.

We may also gather information sent by your browser during your visits to our website or when using the mobile service.

Tracking Technologies and Cookies

AU Lotto uses Cookies and similar technologies to monitor activity and store information. These may include beacons, tags, and scripts designed to analyze and enhance our services. The technologies we use include:

  • Cookies or Browser Cookies. Small files stored on your device. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but this may affect some AU Lotto features.
  • Web Beacons. Tiny files used to count users and track website or email engagement to improve our service.

Cookies can be persistent (stored after you close your browser) or session-based (deleted when you close your browser).

We utilize both session and persistent cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies
    Type: Session Cookies
    Administered by: Us
    Purpose: These are crucial for operating AU Lotto services, authenticating users, and preventing fraud.
  • Cookies for Cookie Consent
    Type: Persistent Cookies
    Administered by: Us
    Purpose: These track your consent to our cookie policy.
  • Functionality Cookies
    Type: Persistent Cookies
    Administered by: Us
    Purpose: These remember your preferences like login details and language to improve your experience.

The Company may use your Personal Data to:

  • Provide and maintain AU Lotto services: including monitoring usage and ensuring service quality.
  • Manage Your Account: to register you as a user and enable access to AU Lotto features.
  • For the performance of a contract: the processing, compliance, and fulfillment of your lottery ticket purchases and any other agreements made with Us through the AU Lotto Service.
  • To contact You: To reach You by email, phone calls, SMS, or equivalent electronic communications such as push notifications from our AU Lotto mobile application regarding updates, draws, winning results, or other relevant information about AU Lotto services.
  • To provide You with news, special offers, and general information: about AU Lotto draws, promotions, new games, and related services that may interest You unless You opt out of receiving such communications.
  • To manage Your requests: To respond to and handle any inquiries, claims, or requests You submit regarding AU Lotto services.
  • For business transfers: Your personal data may be used or transferred in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale involving Our AU Lotto service business assets.
  • For other purposes: To analyze data for improving AU Lotto services, monitor usage trends, evaluate marketing effectiveness, and enhance your overall experience.

    • We may share Your personal information in the following situations:

    • With Service Providers: We may share Your personal information with trusted service providers who assist Us with the operation and management of the AU Lotto service, including user support and communications.

    • With Affiliates: Your information may be shared with Our affiliates that operate AU Lotto services or related businesses, all of whom are required to comply with this Privacy Policy.
    • With business partners: We may share Your information with partners who offer AU Lotto promotions, prizes, or related services.
    • With other users: if You share personal information publicly in AU Lotto forums, chats, or social features, that information may be visible to other users.
    • With Your consent: We may disclose Your personal information for any other purpose with Your explicit consent.

    We retain Your Personal Data only as long as necessary to provide AU Lotto services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.
